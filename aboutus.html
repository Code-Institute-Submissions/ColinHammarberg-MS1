About Us

Refer is a tool for people who find the current system for Covid-19 difficult. It was mainly developed to make everyoneâ€™s lives a little bit easier and to get a good structure on what to do in the current pandemic. Refer offers opportunities for the population of Sweden to schedule their vaccination for the current Virus, order PCR-test without having to go and see a doctor or a nurse & to educate people about the virus. The services are completely free of charge and are being financed by a collaboration with the Swedish Health Department. The only thing required is to create an account and the services offered will directly be available from the service menu in your account. The educational information will also be available when you have registered an account at Refer. You will both receive access to internal videos, written articles & external videos provided by the Health Department Of Sweden. Create your account by clicking the button down below.
